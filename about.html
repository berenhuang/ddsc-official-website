Who We Are

The Deakin Data Science Club (DDSC) is a student-led organization that brings together data enthusiasts, AI researchers, and IT professionals to foster innovation and learning in the field of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence.

  • Workshops and tutorials on Machine Learning, Python, and AI.
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals.
  • Hands-on projects and hackathons for real-world experience.

Our Mission

DDSC's mission is to create a collaborative environment where students can explore the latest advancements in Data Science and AI, gain practical skills, and connect with like-minded peers and professionals.

Our Vision

We aim to be the leading student-led data science community at Deakin University, offering resources, mentorship, and opportunities to help students excel in the industry.
